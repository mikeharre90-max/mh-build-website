About MH Build

Residential construction, done properly.

Our Approach

Every build starts with a solid frame — and a straight answer.

MH Build takes on residential new builds, renovations, extensions, decks, fencing, and project management across Auckland. Every project is treated with the same attention, from the first stud in the ground to the final fix.

We're on site through every stage of the build, not just managing from a distance — so problems get caught early and the finish matches what was promised at the start.

MH Build is run by a Licensed Building Practitioner — so your project is backed by a qualification that's checked, current, and accountable, not just a name on a van.
