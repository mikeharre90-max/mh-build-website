What We Do

Residential construction services.

01 — New Builds

Full residential builds, framed to finish.

From the first stud in the ground to the final handover, we manage the whole build — foundations, framing, weathertightness, and finishing — so it comes together as one solid, well-built home.

02 — Renovations

Reworking homes without cutting corners.

Kitchens, bathrooms, interiors, and full-home renovations — updating what's there while respecting the character of the existing house.

03 — Extensions

Extra space that matches the original.

New rooms, additions, and entryways built to sit naturally alongside your existing home — matched in structure and finish, not bolted on.

04 & 05 — Decks & Fencing

Outdoor spaces built for how you actually live.

Timber decking built for Auckland's weather, plus boundary and privacy fencing — solid, level, and finished neatly, whether it's a pool deck or a simple back fence.

06 — Project Management

Running the job, so you don't have to.

Trades, timelines, materials, and consents all coordinated under one roof — whether it's a build we're delivering end-to-end, or a project you already have underway and need kept on track.

Trade Coordination

Booking and sequencing subcontractors so the right people show up at the right stage, in the right order.

Scheduling

A realistic programme kept up to date, with delays flagged early instead of discovered late.

Consents & Compliance

Council consents, inspections, and paperwork handled properly, so nothing holds up the build.
